Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Description

Having a bad day? Need some comfort food to brighten up your life? Want your family to stuff their faces so you can have a moment of silence? Looking for something quick and easy to cook dinner because this isn't the 1960s where you have all day to cook?

You've come to the right place! This homemade mac and cheese recipe is a fan favorite in my home. The kids love it and I don't want to pull my hair out trying to follow a drawn out recipe rule book to cook it!

Enough with the chit chat. Let's get down to business.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces uncooked white elbow pasta
  • 4 cups water
  • ½ teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper (or to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 8 ounces sharp white cheddar cheese (about 2 cups shredded)
  • 4 ounces sharp cheddar cheese (about 1 cup shredded)
  • ½ cup milk (warmed)

Steps

  1. Place pasta, water, salt, pepper, paprika and garlic powder into the Instant Pot. Stir ingredients well and even out the pasta to make sure it is all submerged.
  2. Close the Instant Pot lid and move the steam release valve to the sealing position. Pressure Cook at high pressure for 4 minutes.
  3. While the pasta is cooking, shred the cheese (if you didn't buy pre-shredded cheese) and warm the milk.
  4. When the cook time ends, quick release the pressure by moving the steam release valve to the venting position.
  5. Once all steam has been released and the pin drops down, open the Instant Pot lid. Add the butter and slowly stir in the cheese. Next, add in the milk.
  6. If the mac and cheese seems too watery, keep it on the warm setting for a few minutes and it will thicken. Taste and serve.
