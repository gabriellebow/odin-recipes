Micki's Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Description

Looking for something easy to wow your dinner guests? Do you have picky eaters in your home? Are you just a huge fan of sweet and sour meatballs? If you've answered yes to any of those questions, then this is the recipe for you! I learned this recipe from my late sister and it has never disappointed.

Enough funny business, this isn't one of those blogs where you scroll forever and a lifetime to get to the ingredients and cooking steps! Let's get to work... and one step closer to eating this delicious meal!

Ingredients

Meatball Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • 1 TSP garlic minced
  • 1 TSP salt
  • 1 TSP black pepper
  • 1 cup onion diced

Sweet and Sour Sauce

  • 1 cup brown sugar packed
  • 1½ cups water
  • ¼ cup white vinegar
  • 3 TBSP all-purpose flour or cornstarch
  • 3 TBSP soy sauce
  • ¼ cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Mix sauce ingredients together in a pot until fully combined
  3. Bring mixture to a rolling boil on low heat and cook until it thickens. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Combine meatball ingredients in a large bowl.
  5. Roll mixture into meatballs and place in baking dish. Bake for about 20 minutes
  6. Remove from oven and drain grease.
  7. Pour sauce over meatballs, covering them completely.
  8. Bake for about 20-30 minutes
  9. Taste and serve with white rice.
